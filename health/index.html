Our Programs

Empowering Communities Through Healthcare & Innovation

At NEA Giant Healthcare, we are committed to transforming lives through medical research, specialized clinics, healthcare support, and climate action. From disease awareness initiatives to free and subsidized healthcare programs, we bring innovative solutions to less privileged communities worldwide.

Centre for Human Disease Research (CHDR)

Raising awareness about critical health issues through initiatives like the Diabetes Awareness Project and Cancer Research Extension Project, in partnership with global institutions.

NEA Medical Camping Program

Bringing healthcare directly to communities by treating multiple cases in rotating locations such as Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and beyond.

NEA Health Care Support Program

Providing essential healthcare supplies like medicines, mosquito nets, and other critical items to support underprivileged communities.

NEA Special Clinics Program

Offering specialized healthcare services through targeted clinics, including eye clinics, cancer screenings, ear treatments, and more.

NEA Climate Change & Environment Conservation Program

Promoting sustainability through tree planting projects, environmental conservation initiatives, and awareness campaigns.

NEA Health Centres

Dedicated to Healthcare Innovation & Climate Action

NEA Giant Healthcare is a non-profit organization focused on advancing healthcare access, disease awareness, medical research, and climate change mitigation in underserved communities.

Our programs provide subsidized medical services, awareness campaigns, and healthcare essentials to improve lives.

We Bring Healthcare Directly to Communities

Our programs operate through mobile medical camps, health centers, specialized clinics, and partnerships with global institutions.

We rotate across Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and beyond, ensuring healthcare reaches those in need.

Subsidized But Not Free

Our clinics are not entirely free, but prices are highly subsidized to make quality healthcare affordable for all.

Registered beneficiaries receive program benefits and priority access to our medical services.
